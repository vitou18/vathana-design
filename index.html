The 7 essential steps to learning Photoshop.

Step 1

Retouch and edit photos like a professional.

Go from dull to stunning by learning how to enhance portraits, products, and more with just a few clicks. You’ll discover tools that make it easy to fix lighting, sharpen details, and bring out the best in any image.

Step 2

Clean up photos and remove distractions with ease.

Remove distractions, retouch skin, and make subtle edits that add polish without overdoing it. These are the small touches that make your work look clean, professional, and ready to share.

Step 3

Blend multiple images to create powerful compositions.

Combine photos, build scenes from scratch, and explore Photoshop’s creative tools for compositing. You’ll learn how to make everything feel like it belongs in the same world.

Step 4

Make your text stand out with professional effects.

From bold titles to gritty textures and glowing neon, you’ll learn how to turn plain type into eye-catching design. Make sure your text always feels like part of the design, not an afterthought.

Step 5

Showcase your designs using real mockups.

Place your work onto objects, clothing, screens, and more. It’s the easiest way to present your designs like a pro and instantly level up your portfolio or client delivery.

Step 6

Export your work the right way for any platform.

No more blurry graphics or wrong formats. You’ll learn exactly how to export for print, web, and social — and which settings to use to keep everything sharp and high quality.

Step 7

Practice with real projects and get expert feedback.

Put everything you’ve learned into action with hands-on design challenges. You’ll build your skills faster with guided projects and personal feedback to help you keep improving.
